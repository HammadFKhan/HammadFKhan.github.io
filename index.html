ABOUT ME

HAMMAD KHAN

Hello! I am a PhD graduate student at Purdue University studying under Dr. Krishna Jayant in the Nano Neurotechnology Lab. My research focuses on technologically driven neuroscience. During my undergraduate, under the guidance of Anja Kunze, I developed a device that investigated the topographical changes that effect neuronal calcium signaling, applicable for neurodegenerative disease. My current research focus extends to using two-photon calcium imaging, high-density electrode recording, and nanofabrication for in vivo application in awake, behaving mice.
